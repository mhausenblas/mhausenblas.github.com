My name is Michael Hausenblas and I work at Red Hat in the OpenShift team. I am a Gopher, a Cloud Native Ambassador and I try to help folks to be successful with container technologies including Docker, CRI-O, Kubernetes, and OpenShift.

Please use m.hausenblas@acm.org for general enquiries.
